Meeting the Three Giants

A night’s walk turned into a lesson from beings larger than life.

The First Giant

He spoke slowly, like stone grinding on stone, and asked what I was building. I said “websites.” He laughed — a laugh that shook the valley.

The Second Giant

He said nothing at all. His silence was a mirror. In it, I heard the noise of my own thoughts.

The Third Giant

He only nodded. The world around him faded into mist. It felt like permission — the path is yours now.
